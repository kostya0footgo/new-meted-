Contact Us
How many photographers and videographers will be at my wedding? +

Our standard wedding package includes: 1-2 photographers (depending on the scale of the event) 1-3 videographers for full video coverage For larger weddings, we recommend adding a second photographer and videographer to ensure no moment is missed. We will discuss the best option for you during our consultation.

Do you use drones for aerial shots? +

Yes! We offer aerial drone photography and videography to capture stunning overhead views of your wedding venue and surroundings. This service is included in select packages or can be added as an extra. Availability depends on weather conditions and venue restrictions.

How do we book your services? +

To book OK Photography US Photo-Videography, follow these steps: 1. Fill out our inquiry form with your event details. 2. We’ll send you a custom proposal based on your needs. 3. Once you're happy with the package, we'll send a contract and invoice for a deposit payment to secure your date. 4. After booking, we’ll discuss your timeline, shot list, and any special requests. We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for peak wedding season dates.

Do you offer engagement sessions? +

Yes! We highly recommend booking an engagement session before your wedding. It’s a great way to get comfortable in front of the camera, capture beautiful pre-wedding moments, and build a connection with our team before the big day. Engagement sessions can be booked separately or as part of your wedding package.

How do we receive our photos and videos? +

All edited photos and videos are delivered via a private online gallery where you can view, download, and share them. Wedding videos are delivered in high-resolution digital format, and we also provide USB drives upon request.

What happens if it rains on my wedding day? +

We always have a backup plan! If it rains, we: Use covered or indoor locations at your venue. Bring professional lighting equipment to ensure great photos and videos in any condition. Capture creative rain shots (if you're open to it!). We also discuss contingency plans with you before the wedding to ensure everything runs smoothly.
